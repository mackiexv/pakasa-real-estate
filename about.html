About Pakasa Real Estate Solutions

We provide Residential and Commercial property solutions and manage real estate properties in the Netherlands. We are specialist providers of Real Estate and Property Management Solutions to local and international investors in the general European market.

Pakasa Real Estate was founded in 2025 by Mark Lubega to provide high quality services across the entire spectrum of property investment. As trusted estate agents, property managers, and advisors to clients and investors, we take pride in our ability to guide you through the entire process.

Our approach to real estate focuses on delivering simplicity, quality, and performance. We understand that our investors and clients want to work with experienced estate agents and industry professionals they trust.

We offer a unique one-stop real estate solution by delivering a comprehensive service to investors, developers, buyers, and sellers. From acquisition and sales of investment property to letting and management of residential and commercial properties in the Netherlands, you are assured of end-to-end support and market leading performance.
